Instructions:

  • ← and → Keys control the speed of the Satellite
  • With ← speeding up and → slowing down
  • ↑ and ↓ Keys control the radius of the Satellites orbit
  • Full 360° rotation via click and drag
  • Zoom by holding Shift + Click and drag
  • Hold Alt (⌥ Opt on Mac) + Click and drag to move along an axis
